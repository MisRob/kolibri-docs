Manage Data
~~~~~~~~~~~

.. note::
  To manage Kolibri users you must sign-in as **Super user** or **Admin**.


You can download Kolibri *Detail* and *Summary* logs usage data and export in the CSV format from the **Data** tab in your **Facility** dashboard.
